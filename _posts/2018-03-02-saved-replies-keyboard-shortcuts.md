For [almost two years](https://github.com/blog/2135-saved-replies) you have been able to use saved replies to quickly respond to multiple issues and pull requests. Now saved replies have keyboard shortcuts to make them even easier to use.

To activate your saved replies press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> <kbd>.</kbd> when composing or replying to an issue or pull request. Select the saved reply of your choice with a number, for example <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> <kbd>2</kbd>.

Note if your saved reply ends with a `#` or `@` after inserting the saved reply it will attempt to autocomplete an issue or pull request number or username. [Check out the documentation to learn more](https://help.github.com/articles/using-saved-replies/).
